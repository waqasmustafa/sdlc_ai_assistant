GETTING STARTED

Up and Running in 4 Steps

Click each step to learn more

1

Open AI Assistant

▼

Click "AI Assistant" in the left sidebar. You'll see the chat interface with conversation history on the left and the chat area on the right.

2

Select AI Model

▼

Choose from the dropdown at the top. Llama 3.3 70B (Smart) is recommended for best accuracy. You can switch anytime.

3

Ask Your Question

▼

Type in plain English and press Enter. Try: "How many contacts?", "Top 5 sales", "Email of John?", "Show all employees"

4

Get Answer & Export

▼

The AI responds with a clear answer. For lists, export as CSV or JSON. Download entire conversations. Rename or delete chats.
